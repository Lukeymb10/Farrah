Farrah The Rabbit

Meet my American Rabbit Farrah,She is five weeks old and loves to hop freely across the floor, eat bunches of cilantro and basi, lay on your shoulder, and most of all, hang out with her best friend gerald! She also loves to chase the dogs around the house, dig large holes, and nibble on fake flowers.
